Available on Meta Quest Store

Skeeball Challenge is a solo-developed VR game built in Unity using C#, released on the Meta Quest Store. The project was designed to capture the satisfying feel of real skee-ball while expanding on the concept with multiple game modes and competitive features tailored for VR.

A major focus of development was achieving realistic ball physics and interaction, ensuring throws feel natural and responsive in VR. The game also features multiplayer support and online leaderboards, allowing players to compete with others and track high scores.

As a solo project, Skeeball Challenge showcases end-to-end development, including gameplay systems, physics tuning, VR interaction, UI, multiplayer integration, and platform release on Meta Quest.
